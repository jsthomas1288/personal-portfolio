Hey! I'm Jacob Thomas

I am a Mechanical Engineer turned Project Engineer turned Front End Developer. My current goals are to land a Front End Developer job, continue to learn and grow by becoming a Full Stack Developer and eventually become a Software Engineer.

I like software development for three reasons - it fits my personality/characteristics, the work environment, and the type of people becoming software developers.

Software development fits my personality/characteristics for the same reason that I wanted to be a Mechanical Engineer; I have a very active imagination and I enjoy creating and innovating things that help people. I've always been fascinated with technology and growing up I believed engineering was the best way I could be involved with technology that helped people. Upon learning about software development I realized this is where the future is headed, my mechanical engineering skills would translate over, and I could use my active imagination to create and innovate technology that helps people.

Based on the information I have received, I've concluded that most technology companies have a forward-thinking mindset and they are creating healthy work environments that increase productivity and give flexibility so that their employees can stay fresh and be able to do more things in life that they enjoy.

In conducting my research and listening to software developers' stories I see all of these ordinary people that are teachers, youth ministers, gas station workers, retail workers, etc. transitioning into tech and transforming their lives for the better which is so inspirational and encouraging. These people are taking the leap of faith into the unknown and finding success in doing so. These are the type of people I want to surround myself with because I want to be a part of a team that has each other's backs and pushes each other to be the best that they can be.

In my spare time, I'm usually spending quality time with family, serving at church, playing soccer, exercising, playing video games, or watching How I Met Your Mother for the 10th time...
